Core service details

Bioburden, sterility, surface swab, stability, laundry, and dishwashing liquid testing.

Bioburden testing is used to check the microbial load in a product, and this test helps monitor microbial presence in raw materials, finished products, or during the manufacturing process. Testing is conducted according to USP 61 and ISO 11737-1:2006.

Sterility testing aims at medical devices labelled as sterile by manufacturers. It checks microbial presence in the product and supports product safety. Sterility testing follows procedures based on USP 71 or ISO 11737-2:1998.

RWL conducts surface swab testing on porous and non-porous surfaces, as well as air monitoring. Swab testing can help check cleaning performance, disinfection procedures, hygiene levels, and bacterial count levels in products, facilities, and public environments.

RWL supports stability testing and shelf-life questions for disinfectants, biocides, wipes, dishwashing liquids, laundry products, and other antimicrobial products where manufacturers need affordable, practical data.

RWL can support dishwashing liquid testing, dishwasher sanitization testing, laundry product testing, antimicrobial textile testing, and bespoke test design where a standard protocol does not fully match the product or customer requirement.
