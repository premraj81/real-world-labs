01

TGA Disinfectant Efficacy Testing

Validate the bactericidal efficacy of disinfectants in accordance with Therapeutic Goods Administration (TGA) requirements, including Option A and Option B testing.

02

EN Standard Disinfectant Testing

Evaluate disinfectant efficacy using recognised European Standards, including EN 1276, EN 13697, EN 13727, and EN 14561.

03

Dishwashing Product Performance Testing

Assess cleaning performance and antimicrobial efficacy of manual dishwashing products using recognised industry methodologies.

04

Cosmetic Product Testing

Support cosmetic product development with microbiology testing, product quality checks, and tailored method advice where appropriate.

05

Bioburden Testing

Check microbial load in raw materials, finished products, and manufacturing processes using recognised microbiology methods.

06

Sterility Testing

Test medical devices and other sterile-labelled products for microbial presence to support product safety and quality requirements.

07

Surface Swab Testing

Surface swabs and environmental monitoring for hygiene checks on benches, equipment, products, and premises.
